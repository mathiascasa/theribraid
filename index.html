— About The RibRaid
Get ready to join an unique adventure. Discover new places, make new memories, live the JetRaid Spirit!

Jet Raid is a jet ski competition established back in 1998 The project started out with eleven jet ski pilots and a handful of volunteers. At 2002 the project evolved into Corona Jet Raid, a sponsorship the helped us become known internationally. Jet Raid has visited and been held in a lot of different environments and places around the world (Senegal, Mali, Mauritania, Gambia, Uruguay, Peru, Greece, Brazil, Argentina). The race is ideal for those who are looking for a physical and mental challenge, looking for fun and are eager to test their endurance to the extreme.
